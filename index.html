About me

Hi, I’m Sara! I’m a curious human who recently switched gears into tech after years of helping run my family’s restaurant. While juggling menus, I discovered I love fixing systems—and making things pretty. That’s what led me to coding: a perfect mix of logic and creativity.I’m passionate about design, especially the kind that feels joyful and intentional. Whether it’s a clean UI, or a cleverly animated button, I get excited about the little details that make an experience memorable. Outside of tech, you’ll usually find me experimenting in the kitchen, getting lost in an art museum, or diving into the ocean with a snorkel and way too much sunscreen. I believe in learning with joy, leading with kindness, and making things that help people (and hopefully make them smile).Now I’m here, building my future—one pixel and one line of code at a time!
